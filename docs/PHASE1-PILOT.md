# Phase 1 Pilot – 2026

## Markets

- Paraguay (Mercosur): 10 farms, target TVL $2M.
- EU/Piedmont: 5 farms, target TVL €1.5M.

## Data & Oracles

- MAG land registry: parcel ID, owner, area, value.
- Satellite NDVI: crop health index, used for insurance triggers.
- REACH list: 27 restricted chemicals, stored on-chain.

## Objectives

- Prove 24h disbursement for compliant farms.
- Reach first $3.5M TVL across both regions.
- Produce data needed for Polygon + Horizon grant reporting.
